Cobalt Blue Spider

The Cobalt blue spider is a tarantula named after the blue color that can be seen on the adult spiders, when looking at them in a certain light, at first glance they can look more dark brown or black. Before Cobalt blue spiders mature they tend to have a more greyish blue color.
